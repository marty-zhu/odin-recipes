Japanese Pickle Cucumbers

What is it?

Simple Japanese pickled cucumbers that goes great with many other traditional dishes. Also great as part of a bento set.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers - 6
  • Rice vinegar - 3 tbsp
  • Japanese soy sauce - 4 tbsp
  • Sugar - 4 tbsp
  • Salt - 2 tsp
  • Hondashi powder - 2 to 3 tsp
  • Sesame oil - up to you!

How to make it?

  1. Cut or slice cucumbers as you please.
  2. Take a mixing bowl and mix all ingredients with processed cucumbers.
  3. Make sure to mix thoroughly, preferably with your hands.
  4. Finished! Enjoy with friends and family!
